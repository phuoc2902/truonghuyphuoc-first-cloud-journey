# Summary Report: "First Cloud Journey Meetup"

### General Event Information
- **Event Name:** First Cloud Journey Meetup
- **Date & Time:** 09:00 - 12:00, June 13, 2026
- **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City
- **Role:** Attendee

### Presentations & Main Highlights
1. **Architecture Flow for Building a URL Shortener using AWS Services**
   - **Details:** The first group shared the system architecture and operational flow to construct a URL shortener website. The speakers explained how to integrate and configure various AWS services to ensure stability, cost optimization, and high scalability.
2. **Bridging the Gap Between Data Skills and Real-World Business Challenges**
   - **Details:** A highly practical session from the second speaker on applying data skills to solve actual business problems. In addition, he provided essential career advice for landing technology roles at Multinational Corporations (MNCs).
3. **Corporate Culture Fit in the Digital Era**
   - **Details:** The speaker emphasized the importance of corporate culture. While tech professionals often obsess over cloud architectures, DevOps pipelines, and LeetCode scores, MNC hiring decisions are heavily influenced by cultural fit rather than just who writes the cleanest code.
4. **Supporting and Sharing AWS Knowledge with University Students**
   - **Details:** This session focused on mentoring and transferring cloud knowledge to college students. The initiative helps them build foundational cloud skills, making them job-ready for the industry.
5. **DevOps Real-World Experience and Traps to Avoid**
   - **Details:** The final speaker shared hands-on experiences working as a DevOps engineer. He introduced popular frameworks applied in the industry and discussed common mistakes and pitfalls to avoid for smooth CI/CD pipelines and systems operation.

### Key Takeaways & Action Plan
#### New Knowledge & Mindsets
- **Technical Skills:** Understood the production workflow of a URL Shortener on AWS, alongside DevOps best practices and pitfalls to avoid in systems administration.
- **Business-Oriented Mindset:** Gained a clearer perspective on applying technical data skills to address actual business pain points.
- **Professional Growth:** Recognized that cultural fit and soft skills play a decisive role in career success, particularly in multinational environments.

#### Action Plan
- Leverage AWS services to build minor tools like a URL shortener for practice.
- Balance technical training with communication, teamwork, and learning about multinational corporate cultures.
- Incorporate DevOps checklist guidelines to prevent deployment mistakes in personal workshops/projects.
